In the play were the blood mixes we are introducded to a character named mooch. Mooch is of indigenous descent and has had experiences in a residential school.” Mooch there he had it real bad. Hell, ya mooch you will be loaded”. I think his experiences has changed him for the worst as in the play as his name would imply he is a mooch or a parasite that feeds of others and does really take care of himself.

His girlfriend June is always mad at him because he steals her money to buy himself drinks with his friends, not only that but he gets his friends to buy him beers saying he will buy the next one. I think his experiences in the residential school never got explored and he has not been able to put his missing pieces back together.
